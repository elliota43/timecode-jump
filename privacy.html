Privacy Policy — Timecode Jump

Last updated: 2026-01-02

Overview

Timecode Jump is a browser extension that lets you enter a timecode and jump the current page’s HTML5 video to that time.

Data collection

Timecode Jump does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local storage

For convenience, Timecode Jump may store the last-entered timecode locally in your browser (e.g., to prefill the input next time). This data stays on your device and is not sent anywhere.

Permissions

The extension runs on pages you use so it can locate the page’s <video> element and set its playback time. The activeTab permission is used to target the currently active tab when you trigger the extension shortcut.
